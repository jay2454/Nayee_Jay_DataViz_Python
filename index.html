Overall Comparison between Australia, Germany and USA.

The Olympic Games is an important international event featuring summer and winter sports. Over time the Olympics have become bigger and bigger and here you are seeing the overall comparison between some big countries like Germany, Australia and USA.

Total numbers of Gold medals win by Australia, Germany and USA .

Here you can see that USA is in the lead but Germany is also giving great competition to USA and Australia won less medals than other both countries.

Total numbers of Silver medals win by Australia, Germany and USA .

The USA is still in the lead and giving great competition to Germany and Australia

Total numbers of Bronze medals win by Australia, Germany and USA .

Again USA is still on the lead but still Germany has won more medals than Australia.

Total Number of medals won by Men and Women in Germany.

The issues of gender are often controversial and it is difficult to say whether a greater focus on male sports is beneficial or undesirable. What is true is that young boys and girls should be given the opportunity to play sport to be healthy and potentially successful, but that success needs to be determined by what people want to see, not what they are told they should watch.
